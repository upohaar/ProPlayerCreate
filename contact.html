Frequently Asked Questions

How do I hire players for my team?

Browse our player database, review profiles and statistics, then use the "Add to Team" button to recruit players within your budget constraints.

What's the maximum team size?

Teams can have up to 10 players, including substitutes. Your budget will determine how many top-tier players you can recruit.

Can players contact me directly?

Yes! Players can send recruitment requests through our platform, and you'll be notified via email.

Is there a subscription fee?

Basic team management is free. Premium features including advanced analytics and priority support are available with our Pro subscription.
